Quixotic

Definition
unrealistically idealistic or exceedingly impractical pursuits, often driven by noble but unrealistic goals.
Synonyms Antonyms
Idealistic Practical
Unrealistic Realistic
Dreamy Pragmatic
Example
He launched a quixotic campaign to end poverty single-handedly, despite lacking resources or support.
Her quixotic dreams of becoming a famous poet in a week made everyone smile with concern.
