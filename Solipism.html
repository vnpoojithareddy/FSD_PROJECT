Solipsism

Definition
only one's own mind is certain to exist, and everything outside of it (including other people and the external world) might be an illusion or unknowable.
Synonyms Antonyms
Egoism Realism
Subjectivism Objectivism
Example
In his essay, the philosopher argued that believing only your own mind exists is a dangerous form of solipsism.
Her constant need to center every conversation around herself bordered on emotional solipsism.
